Bringing Agile Architecture into the Business Mainstream.

Our customers partner with us to bring their Enterprise Systems or their Software and Telecommunications product offerings to the leading edge of Cloud Computing, Mobile and Agile Development.

The radical demands on User Experience, Accessibility, Extensibility and Scalability of applications at web-scale have given rise to a host of powerful technologies and architectural principles and global services with hundreds of millions of users couldn't exist without them. Following the lead of Amazon, internet companies also started offering access to their own platform As A Service, ushering in the Cloud Computing era. The benefits that drove adoption of Agile Architecture on the Internet are equally relevant to Enterprise computing. Increasingly powerful platforms automate more of the plumbing involved in setting up services and developing apps, freeing developers to focus their attention on the business problem instead and allowing effective and flexible utilisation of investments in infrastructure. Systems built through loose integration of fine-grained services are flexible to quick iteration as business requirements change. We are dedicated to bringing these benefits to the mainstream of Enterprise computing.
